-Student Representative Council

In 2020 I start involve and chosen as student representative council. If you wonder who/what is student representative concil, basically the Students' Representative Council (SRC) consists of 12 council members from the various Faculty of the University. These Council members are elected through a democratic election held every year. The Council has 6 major portfolios namely Clubs and Societies, Event Management, Student Affairs, Publicity, Public Relations, Sports and Recreation, which collectively contribute to the main goals of the Council. In SRC, my portfolio is as Executive Council of Multimedia.
